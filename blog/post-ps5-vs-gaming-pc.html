PS5 vs Custom Gaming PC — which makes sense in 2026?

Apr 22, 2026· 5 min read· By Rajesh at Mahesh Electronics

Every week someone walks into the shop asking this exact question. They've got ₹60k–₹1.5L to spend, they want to play the new games, and they can't tell if ₹54,990 on a PS5 or ₹1.3 lakh on a custom rig is the smarter move. Here's the honest version we give them at the counter.

The quick answer

If you just want to play AAA games on a TV and don't care about anything else, the PS5 wins on every metric that matters — price, ease, exclusives like Spider-Man 2 and God of War Ragnarök. If you also want to study, work, stream, mod games, or upgrade piece-by-piece, a custom PC wins. That's the whole article, but read on for the actual trade-offs.

Price: it's not as close as you think

A PS5 Slim Disc is ₹54,990. Add a second controller (₹5,990), a year of PS Plus (₹4,990), and you're at about ₹66,000 ready to play. You already have a TV.

A custom PC that actually beats the PS5 in performance starts at ₹1.05–1.30 lakh once you add a 1440p monitor, keyboard, mouse and headset. The gap is ₹50,000+ before you've bought a single game.

If your budget is ₹70,000 or below and gaming is the whole point, get the PS5. A cheap PC at that price will disappoint you.

Game library

  • PS5 exclusives — Spider-Man 2, God of War Ragnarök, Demon's Souls, Returnal, Gran Turismo 7. These don't exist on PC (or arrive years later).
  • PC exclusives — PUBG, Valorant, CS2, Dota 2, Minecraft Java, modded Skyrim, Roblox, most MMOs, every strategy game worth playing.
  • Cross-platform — GTA, FIFA/FC, COD, Elden Ring — available on both.

If you're a student

Get the PC. You'll use it for projects, coding, video editing, and it'll last 5–6 years with a single GPU upgrade. The PS5 becomes an expensive paperweight once college assignments start piling up.

If you already own a laptop

Get the PS5. You've got the productivity side covered. The PS5 gives you couch gaming on the TV — a totally different experience from laptop gaming.

What we'd actually recommend in Valsad

  • Budget ₹55–70k, just gaming → PS5 Slim Disc
  • Budget ₹80k–1L, multi-use → Gaming laptop (HP Victus, Lenovo Legion)
  • Budget ₹1.2L+, serious enthusiast → Custom PC we build for you
  • Family with kids → PS5 (no driver issues, no malware, no tantrums about GPU drivers)
