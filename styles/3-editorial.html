Forty years of bringing the future to Valsad.

From the first cassette player on Halar Road to South Gujarat's only PlayStation 5 hub — Mahesh Electronics has quietly authored this town's relationship with technology since 1985.

By · Rajesh & Jeetesh GajaraniReading Time · 4 minEst · 1985

Feature

One shop. Four decades. Eighteen authorized brands.

When Mahesh Gajarani opened a small radio repair counter opposite Valsad's main post office in 1985, the word "laptop" didn't yet exist in most Indian homes. Forty years later, the same shop sells PlayStation 5s to Gen-Z and supplies complete government computer labs through GeM.

Today the counter carries HP, Lenovo, Dell, Canon, Hikvision, D-Link, Epson, Brother, Asus — eighteen partnerships in all — and has become the default IT answer for doctors, architects, schools and Zilla offices across South Gujarat.
